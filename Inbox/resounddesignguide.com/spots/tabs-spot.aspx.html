Tabs-spot

The tabs spot is used to display different spots within tabs.

1: Tabs. The spots consist of tabs. In every tab you create your spots.

2: Spot container. In every tab you'll find a spot container. You can add any sort, and any number, of spot types here.

Image 1


Image 2 - Editing spots:

When in edit mode you won't see the spots for the tabs spot. What you do is simply click on the tab where you want to edit or add a spot, then click "edit panel spots".Â This will open a new window where you can insert spots, just like you normally would.


Image 3 - Add tabs:

If you want to add a new tab, simply click on the tab spot (click a blank area within the tab spot), then click the + button.
