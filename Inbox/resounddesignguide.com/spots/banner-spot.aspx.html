Banner-spot

LIVE demo

The product page starts with a pitch area that describes the unique selling proposition – USP - of this product. We recommend that the pitch area consist of only one “slide” - describing exactly this USP.

Use the creative concept of the product to communicate why the product is exceptional in a simple, elegant and to-the-point USP.

The pitch area is always followed by the “Key messages” section, where the benefits (other selling points) of the product are described. Conceptually it is about telling a structured, prioritized story about the product that should enable the dispenser to pitch the product to his customers. (If a country decides to divide the long product page into several pages, “Pitch area” and “Key messages” should always figure together on the same page)

The pitch area is a dynamic section, which can change in both content and flow over time and in accordance with points product launch, feature pushes, campaigns, etc.

 

Format

  • Image – shows the product in a live scenario that has people as well as the product in it
  • Headline – Product brand key message
  • Copy – Ideally just one paragraph describing what is unique about this product
  • Call to action (the grey box) - But only if relevant (in connection with a campaign etc.)
  • A video or flash elements (which conjunct with size parameters) can also be embedded.

Size/parameters:

Images used should be Width: 736px, Height: 347px.

Format should be PNG.
