Productlink-spot

LIVE demo

Using the productlink spots gives the visitor a brief overview of the product lines. The whole spot is clickable.

 

The spot consists of:

  • Image - W: 231px, H: 120px
  • Link
  • Header
  • Text


Image note

It is recommended that the images fit the exact sizes, as defined above. That way you can position the product itself within that area.
