DropdownLink-spot

LIVE demo

The dropdown spot is used to give the visitor a choice between several links from a dropdown menu.

It consists of a:

  • Header
  • Dropdown (list of links)
  • Image (Recommended: Transparent PNG - W: 316px, H: 163px)

The style sheet will force the image to fit the maximum width of the spots grid, regardless of the actual image size. If the image is smaller than the spot itself, it will be aligned to the bottom right.Â 

Don't upload larger images than needed. Large images make the website slow.
