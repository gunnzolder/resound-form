Promotion-spot

LIVE demo

The promotion spot is used to make small teaser spots that a visitor can click. The visitor is redirected to the wanted destination.

It consists of a:

  • Header
  • Text
  • Link
  • Image (Recommended: Transparent PNG - W: 316px, H: 163px)

The style sheet will force the image to fit the maximum width of the spots grid, regardless of the actual image size. If the image is smaller than the spot itself, it will be aligned to the bottom right. 

Don't upload larger images than needed. Large images make the website slow.

When hovering over the spot, it has a mouseover effect demonstrating that the entire spot is clickable.

 

Example code

<div class="spot">
	<div class="context promo">
		<a href="resound-verso">
			<h2>A more natural sound experience</h2>
			<div class="imgContainer">
				<img src="image-url" alt="bird">
			</div>
			<div class="textContainer">			
				<div class="mainText">
					ReSound Verso™ uses our most 
					<br>advanced technology to let 
					you <br>engage naturally with
					the world <br>around you.<br>
				</div>								
			</div>
			<div class="link">ReSound Verso</div>
		</a>
	</div>
</div>
