Typografi

3 styles are used on the site:

  • Helvetica Neue is used in h1 and h2, as well as on buttons and in the left menu
  • Verdana is used in various spots and in the left menu
  • Arial is used in various spots, the left menu and h3-h6

Depending on the level in the left menu, different fonts are applied.

Helvetica Neue comes in 2 versions: A light, which is used for h1, and a medium.
